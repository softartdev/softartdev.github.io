About Me

Artur Babichev

I’m the developer behind softartdev and Soft Art Development, a one-person studio focused on mobile and multiplatform software.

How I Work

I work mostly with Kotlin, Android, and Kotlin Multiplatform. The projects I enjoy most sit at the intersection of product work and reusable engineering: apps, libraries, and the delivery tooling around them.

Soft Art Development is the public-facing name I use for that solo practice. It keeps the site a little more studio-like, while this page keeps the personal context visible.

I’m based in Tbilisi, Georgia. Public work usually lands first in open-source repositories, where I can iterate on architecture, UI, and build flow before deciding what belongs in commercial work later.
