About

I build mobile and multiplatform software.

Background

I started learning Java in 2015, began building for Android in 2016, and moved to Kotlin about a year and a half later. Along the way, I also had some experience on the iOS side with Objective-C and Swift, although Android remained my main focus.

Later my work shifted toward Kotlin Multiplatform: first for Android and iOS, then for desktop, and later for the web as Compose Multiplatform matured.

This site is a compact overview. The portfolio goes into more detail on the projects I have worked on across different platforms.

Interests

Alongside product development, I am also interested in cybersecurity and related technical topics.

The blog, GitHub profile, and published apps are the best places to see what I am building, testing, and writing about in more detail.
